What Pops in my head

Creating Forgiving User Experience (UX)

It is always a good idea to focus on the core functionality of a system, it guides you from getting distracted from the actual aim or purpose of that design. However, most designers get it all wrong, they do not consider the fact users are bound to make mistakes (I mean, we all are). This punishes the user by forcing them to repeat a whole process all over which is very frustrating if you ask me.
